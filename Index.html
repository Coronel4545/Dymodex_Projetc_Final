What is Dymodex?


Dymodex is Decentralized Exchange that aims to implement and merge functions that are only found in CEX.
As a launch platform for tokens and Nfts. We also intend to implement a token purchase system using fiat currency, buy limit and market orders as well as on CEX.
All to make a DEX more comfortable and versatile to wear. After all, Dex has a high potential to surpass CEX in the near future.

Why did we choose Manticora as a symbol of DEX ?

We chose the mythological manticore because it has a wide range of offensive and defensive systems. Be fully capable of adapting and solving problems.
We believe that the Manticora is the mythological animal that best represents, in the literal sense, the Dymodex proposal. Which is to merge existing DEX and CEX resources into a single Dapp!
